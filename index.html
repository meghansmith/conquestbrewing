About Us

Conquest Brewing Company combines an understanding of microbiology, chemistry, and physics with a passion for brewing different and exciting beers. Established in 2012, we are Columbia, SC’s first production craft brewery since the prohibition. Our brewery is located just blocks from Williams Brice stadium, and includes a tasting room. Come and have a drink with us 7 days a week!

“Conquest Brewery is one of Columbia's hidden treasures. Their tasting room offers a varied assortment of craft beers certain to please the palates of both the novice and experienced beer lover. The atmosphere is casual and welcoming and the guys behind the bar are quick to share both their knowledge of the brewing process and , even more, their love of unique, finely crafted beer with the customers." ~ Doug Ross via Facebook

Our Team

Our President and Head Brewer, Joseph Ackerman, is a Columbia native whose interest in brewing started while attending USC. He got involved with the Palmetto State Brewers Association, where he renewed his friendship with high school classmate Matthew Ellisor.

Their variety is outsanding for a small operation - often having eight of their beers ready. Even their simpler styles like the brown ale have plenty of flavor and depth." ~Christopher K via Yelp

Matthew Ellisor is the Vice President and Assistant Brewer, and has received an education in Brewing Technology from the Siebal Institute of Technology. Another Columbia native, he has found the perfect melding of his technical brewing skills and passion for flavorful beers in Conquest Brewing Company.

"This place churns out some tasty brews that range from a lighter blonde to the inferno that they call the Imperial Bipolar High Roller, which is brewed with ghost peppers. Some of my favorites are the Seven Seas Ale and the Artemis Blonde." ~ April B via Yelp
